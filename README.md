# Pollution Awareness & Solutions Web App

A clean, informative, multi-page web application designed to educate users on the definitions, causes, effects, and solutions of environmental pollution. The website features interactive links, multimedia elements, and a dedicated case study on urban pollution focusing on Lahore, Pakistan.

---

##  Features

* **Educational Content:** Clear explanations of what pollution is, along with its physical and intangible forms.
* **Multimedia Integration:** Embedded video resources ("Learn about Pollution") directly on the homepage to enhance user engagement.
* **Structured Resource Breakdown:** Categorized bulleted sections outlining the primary causes and effects of pollution (Air quality, Water resources, Soil health).
* **Deep-Dive Case Study:** A dedicated segment addressing the severe air pollution crisis in Lahore, detailing its impact on public health and daily life.
* **Actionable Solutions:** Comprehensive strategies for pollution control, highlighting both large-scale industrial measures and everyday individual habits.
* **Responsive Navigation:** Includes anchor tags and a structured internal linking system (`Back to Home`, `Solutions`, etc.) for seamless page navigation.
